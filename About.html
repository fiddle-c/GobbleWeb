About

Here’s a bit about the faces behind Gobble: We’re a ragtag team of three childhood friends from a small town in Southern California looking to break into the industry autonomously. We are all first generation graduates and come from backgrounds a plethora of our peers do not. We hope to show the world that anyone could create an impactful application, no matter who you are or where you are located.
