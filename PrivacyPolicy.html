Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 8, 2025

The Recipe Exchange values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information in connection with our mobile application and related services.

1. Information We Collect

  • Profile Information: username, display name, and profile image
  • Recipe Information: titles, ingredients, instructions, and images
  • Technical Information: usage data, crash logs, and device info
  • Authentication Information: managed using Firebase Authentication

2. How We Use Information

  • To provide core app features like creating and sharing recipes
  • To personalize user profiles and social interactions
  • To maintain and improve performance and security
  • To communicate about important updates or support

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent personal data. We use trusted third-party services, including:

  • Firebase (Authentication, Firestore, Storage)
  • Firebase Crashlytics for stability and performance monitoring

4. Data Retention & User Controls

Your data is stored as long as your account remains active. You may request account deletion at any time by contacting us at kflor@gobbleapp.io. When deleted, your profile and recipe data are permanently removed.

5. Security

We use industry-standard security measures and Firebase security rules to protect your data. However, no system can guarantee 100% security.

6. Children’s Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If such data is discovered, it is deleted promptly.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Updates will be posted here with a new “Last updated” date.
